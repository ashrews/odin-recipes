Chicken Curry

Using only a few ingredients, you can make this delishous spaghetti.

  • olive oil
  • small onion, diced
  • 2 pounds of chicken thighs, cut into large cubes
  • 1 15 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1.5 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1.5 tsp paprika
  • 1.5 tsp coriander
  • 1.5 tsp tumuric
  1. First pour about 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a pot on medium heat.
  2. While the oil is heating up, dice an onion and stir into the olive oil. Add chicken. Stir intermittently until fully cooked
  3. Add the humburger meat. Stir intermittently until meat is browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and tomato paste and stir thoroughly.
