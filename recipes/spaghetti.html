Spaghetti

Using only a few ingredients, you can make this delishous spaghetti.

  • olive oil
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 large can of tomato paste
  • 1 large can of tomato sauce
  • 1 large onion
  1. First pour about 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a pot on medium heat.
  2. While the oil is heating up, dice an onion and stir it with the olive oil.
  3. Add the humburger meat. Stir intermittently until meat is browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and tomato paste and stir thoroughly.
